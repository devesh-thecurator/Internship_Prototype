Investment Term Sheet

Payment: Investor will fund INR 25,000,000 in two tranches after completion of diligence and board approval.

Governing Law: This term sheet and related definitive agreements are governed by the laws of India, with courts in New Delhi having jurisdiction.

Confidentiality: Each party must keep commercial terms, diligence material, and customer information confidential for three years.

Termination: Either party may terminate negotiations if definitive agreements are not executed within 60 days or if diligence identifies a material adverse issue.

Liability: Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential damages, except for fraud, wilful misconduct, or confidentiality breaches.

Compliance: The issuer confirms that proceeds will be used for lawful business purposes and that sanctions, anti-bribery, and KYC checks will be completed before closing.
